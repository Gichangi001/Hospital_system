Cancer Center

The Mater Hospital Nairobi is committed to helping people beat cancer by providing comprehensive and multidisciplinary cancer care services. Here are some ways in which the hospital supports patients in their battle against cancer:

Expert Oncology Team: The hospital has a team of highly skilled oncologists, surgeons, radiation therapists, nurses, and support staff who specialize in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of various types of cancers. These professionals work together to develop personalized treatment plans tailored to each patient's unique needs.

Advanced Diagnostic Services: The Mater Hospital Nairobi is equipped with state-of-the-art imaging technologies, such as MRI, CT scan, PET scan, and digital mammography, among others. These advanced diagnostic tools help in early and accurate cancer detection, allowing for timely intervention.

Comprehensive Treatment Options: The hospital offers a wide range of treatment options, including surgery, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, immunotherapy, targeted therapy, and hormone therapy. The oncology team evaluates each patient's condition and recommends the most appropriate treatment modality or a combination of therapies to achieve the best possible outcomes.

Multidisciplinary Tumor Boards: The Mater Hospital Nairobi conducts regular multidisciplinary tumor boards where oncologists, surgeons, radiologists, pathologists, and other specialists come together to review complex cancer cases. This collaborative approach ensures that patients receive well-informed treatment decisions based on the collective expertise of the entire team.

Supportive Care Services: Battling cancer can be physically and emotionally challenging. The hospital provides a range of supportive care services to help patients cope with the side effects of treatment, manage pain, and maintain their overall well-being. These services may include pain management, palliative care, psychological support, nutrition counseling, and survivorship programs.

Clinical Trials and Research: The Mater Hospital Nairobi actively participates in clinical trials and research studies to advance cancer treatment and contribute to medical knowledge. By offering patients the opportunity to participate in cutting-edge research, the hospital expands treatment options and improves outcomes for both current and future cancer patients.

Patient Education and Support: The hospital places great emphasis on patient education and empowerment. They provide educational resources, support groups, and counseling services to help patients and their families understand the disease, make informed decisions, and navigate the cancer journey with confidence.

Survivorship Programs: The Mater Hospital Nairobi recognizes that cancer treatment doesn't end with the completion of therapy. They offer comprehensive survivorship programs that focus on follow-up care, monitoring for recurrence, and promoting long-term survivorship. These programs aim to enhance the quality of life for cancer survivors and provide ongoing support throughout their journey.

The Mater Hospital Nairobi's dedicated oncology team, advanced treatment options, multidisciplinary approach, supportive care services, and commitment to research all contribute to their efforts in helping people beat cancer. By providing comprehensive and compassionate care, they strive to improve outcomes and offer hope to individuals and families affected by cancer.
