Cancer

Our four year Cancer Program is oriented to support sustain all modern and known cancer challenges. We aim to teach people not just how to deploy cutting edge tools and techniques, but how to build the next generation of medical experties . This programme aims to give a well integrated balance of theoretical underpinnings and practical experience, strongly informed by the research expertise of our Medical staff.
